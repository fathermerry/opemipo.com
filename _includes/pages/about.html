My name is Opemipo Aikomo.

I was born in Lagos, Nigeria and I've lived here most of my life. My parents and two siblings also live in Lagos.

I make things and tell stories on the internet. My most recent project is a collection of publishing experiments called Wuruwuru.

I'm a self-taught web and software designer. I learnt most of what I know on the internet and on the job. Many of my personal and professional friendships were also made online. Quite literally, the internet saved my life.

I'm very curious and always excited to learn. My biggest interests are design, typography, history, archiving, mixology, psychology, economics, art and technology in general.

I try to be as many things as I can.
