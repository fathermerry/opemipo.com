My name is Opemipo Aikomo.

I was born in Lagos, Nigeria. I grew up in Liasu with my parents and two siblings, moved around for school and ended up studying Computer Engineering at Obafemi Awolowo University.

I'm a self-taught web and software designer. I make things and tell stories on the internet. My most recent project is a collection of publishing experiments called Wuruwuru.

The internet quite literally saved my life. Many of my personal and professional relationships were made on Twitter and I consider myself a first-citizen of the web.

I'm very curious and always excited to learn. I love design, typography, history, comics, animation, archiving, mixology, psychology, economics, art and technology in general.

I try to be as many things as I can.
