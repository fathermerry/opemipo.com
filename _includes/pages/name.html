My full name is Onaopemipo Aikomo.

Onaopemipo means "I have many reasons to thank god" and Aikomo translates to "we don't reject children". They're both Yoruba names from Western Nigeria.

Most people call me Ope. The letter "p" in Yoruba is a labial-velar consonant so it's pronounced "or-kpe".
